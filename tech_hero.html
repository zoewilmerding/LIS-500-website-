Our Tech Hero: Kimberly Bryant

Founder of Black Girls CODE • Electrical Engineer • Tech Equity Advocate

Kimberly Bryant is an American electrical engineer and the founder of Black Girls CODE, a nonprofit launched in 2011 that empowers girls of color to learn computer programming and pursue careers in technology. She is widely recognized for advocating diversity, access, and representation in STEM.
